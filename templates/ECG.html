  • Basics: Theory and concepts of a healthy EEG signal.

This process includes the download of open-source marked recordings, database pre-processing, architecture optimization, model training, and model evaluation. The elaboration of the network was developed step by step and it explained how to identify overfitting and how to correct it. The final accuracy of the algorithm was 81%.
